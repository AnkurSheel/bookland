<h2 id="why-discoveries-in-bookland">Why Discoveries in Bookland</h2>
<p>I realized that although I was reading a lot of books with a lot of takeaways, I was forgetting the takeaways after a while. This is an attempt to pen down my thoughts about the key ideas of the book.</p>
<h2 id="structure">Structure</h2>
<ul>
<li>The book I read</li>
<li>A short summary of the book.</li>
<li>Key Takeaways</li>
<li>A recommendation of whether I thought it was worth my time</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="join-me-on-the-discovery">Join me on the discovery</h2>
<p>Here, I endeavor to put down the key ideas from the books I read. Every book you will see is a book I have actually read.</p>
<p>In the weeks that follow I'll be writing up posts for this series as I finish each book.</p>
<p>I hope you join me and I would love to hear your thoughts :)</p>
